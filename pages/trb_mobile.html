TRB Mobile App (Mobile Banking)

Our Mobile Banking service is an electronic banking application designed to bring basic banking access and functionality to our customers on their mobile phones. With our Mobile Banking App, you can bank anywhere, anytime without restrictions.

All you need is a mobile phone and access to internet.

TRB Mobile App service is currently available on Android and iOS application.

Target Market

§  Individual customers

Features:

·        Balance Enquiry

·        Transaction List (Last 10 transactions)

·        View Exchange rate

·        Funds Transfer to TRB and other bank account)

·        TRB Money Vouchers

·        Mobile Top up (All Networks)

·        Bill Payments (ECG, DSTV/GOTV, Surfline etc.)

·        Mobile Money Transactions

·        Cheque Book Request

·        Stop Cheque

·        Pin Request

·        Stop Card

Benefits:

§  Check account balances, and make bill payments, transfer money and organize your accounts in real time anywhere you can access internet

§  Track investments regardless of your location.

§  Allows 24/7 access to account.

§  Talk and browse unlimited by topping up any time you run out of airtime right from your phone.

§  Send money to a mobile money wallet

§  Secured: TRB uses strong encryption to safeguard and protect customer’s privacy, identity and financial information. SMS verification code is use to authorize payments for added security.

How do I access TRB Mobile App

·        Visit the nearest TRB branch, fill a simple application form.

·        Username will be sent via email address and Password via SMS

·        Download App from Play Store or App store

·        Log in with TRB Mobile username and password
