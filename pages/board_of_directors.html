  • PROF. BILL BUENAR PUPLAMPU
    Board Chairman

    Prof. Bill Buenar Puplampu is an academic and practicing Psychologist with specialization in Organizational and Occupational Psychology. He has several years of experience in research, teaching, consulting and practice in Organizational Psychology. He has expertise in Corporate Governance, Human Resource Management and Organizational Behaviour. He started his academic career in UK at the University of Westminster and East London and later joined the University of Ghana and Central University. He has been involved in several consulting projects including Human Capital Solutions for PricewaterhouseCoopers in Ghana and other Government assignments. He is the founding Director of PsyconHR. He has served on the Academic Board, Board of Graduate Studies and Faculty Board of University of Ghana Business School. He also served as Independent Director on the Board of Merchant Bank Ghana Ltd. and the Board of Regents University College and Central University. He currently serves on the Board of Databank Education Investment Fund and Ghana Psychology Council.

    Prof. Puplampu holds a Doctorate Degree in Organizational Behaviour and International Human Resource Management from University of London, MSc. CNAA from Polytechnic of East London in Organisational Psychology and BA (Hons.) in Psychology with Sociology from University of Ghana.

    He belongs to the following professional bodies; British Psychological Society; Academy of Management; Africa Academy of Management; International Association of Applied Psychology and Ghana Psychology Council.

    He has attended various courses and workshops internally and outside the country. He has several papers and articles accredited to him.
  • OSEI ASAFO - ADJEI
    Member

    Mr. Osei Asafo-Adjei was until 30th June 2016, Executive Director, Corporate Banking Division of HFC Bank Ltd. He previously worked in senior management positions with The Trust Bank as General Manager, Business Development, Head Corporate Banking and Head, Corporate and Commercial Banking between January 2004 and May 2012. He started his banking career as a Credit Officer with Agricultural Development Bank (ADB) in July 1993. He has attended several course, seminars and Executive Development Programs on Global Trade Finance, Corporate Finance, Trade & Investment, and Customer Care Relations in Portugal, South Africa, Turkey, Belgium among others. Mr. Asafo-Adjei holds a Master of Philosophy degree in Agricultural Economics from the University of Glasgow, Scotland, a Diploma in Development Studies from University of Cambridge, England and a Bachelor of Science in Agricultural Economics from the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Kumasi-Ghana. 
  • VICTORIA EMEAFA HARDCASTLE
    Member

    Mrs. Emeafa Hardcastle is a Lawyer by profession. Her career spans 21 years with extensive experience in Corporate and Commercial Law practice. She has worked as a private legal practitioner as well as in the Banking Industry. She has also worked as Deputy Head, Economic Section, Economic Trade attaché of the Swiss Embassy. She had a stint with the Hogan and Lovell, UK under International Lawyers for Africa program. She also has a wealth of experience in the Energy Sector. She served on the Board of Petroleum Commission. She also undertakes consultancy services on Corporate Law.

    Mrs. Hardcastle holds a LLM in Banking and Finance from the London School of Economics and Political Science and a BA Hons, Law and Sociology from the University of Ghana Legon. She is a senior member of the Ghana Bar Association. She was awarded the FKA Apaloo prize for Company Law whiles at the Ghana School of Law.
  • SAMUEL KWADJO AGYAPONG APPENTENG
    Member

    Mr. Samuel Kwadjo Agyapong Appenteng is an Engineer by profession. He is also a successful Entrepreneur. He has in-depth industry and business leadership experience gained from various capacities as Director, Technical Services and General Manager of Intravenous Infusions Ltd. He is currently the Managing Director of Joisson Gh. Ltd. He has served on the Boards of AGI Consult, Advisory Board, Ghana Universities Press, Ministerial Advisory Committee on Institutional Reforms of Urban Water Sector and Methodist University College.

    Mr. Appenteng holds an MBA Finance and BSc. Chemical Engineering from University of Leicester, UK and also a graduate from KNUST, Kumasi. He is a corporate member of the Ghana Institution of Engineers, a fellow of the African Leadership Initiative West Africa and an affiliate of Aspen Institute of Colorado,USA.
  • KWAKU OTEMPONG
    Member

    Mr. Kwaku Otempong is a Chartered Accountant by profession and has several years of work experience from both the private and public sector of the country. He worked with SSB Bank now SG Bank in the following capacities; Manager in Charge of Revenue, Head of Budget and Chief Accountant. He also performed relieving duties as Deputy Managing Director in the absence of the substantive Deputy Managing Director. He also worked as Finance Officer at Ahmadu Bello University in Nigeria. Other positions he has held include Auditor Senior/Accountant at Conn. & Co. London, UK and Articled Clerk with Ayew Agyemang Turkson and Co., Accra. He has served as Chairman of SSB Forex Bureau, SG PF Board of Trustees and as Director on the Board of Asubonten Rural Bank and Consumer Credit Ltd. He also served as a member of ACCA Committee, Ghana Branch, Honorary Auditor, Charismatic Baptist Church and is a Director of Reko Catering Services.

    Mr. Otempong is a product of London School of Accountancy and Henley Management College UK now Henley Business School, University of Reading, UK. He has a Masters Degree in Business Administration, an Associate member of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants, UK (Accra) and a Fellow of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (FCCA).
  • EYRAM KWAME ATSU
    Member

    Mr. Eyram Kwame Atsu is a Banking and Business Development Consultant by profession and has Thirty-four (34) years of working experience in Banking from Standard Chartered Bank in Corporate Banking and Credit. His roles spanned from Credit Analyst, Relationship Manager, and Regional Corporate Manager in charge of Northern Ghana. He was responsible for Corporate Banking and Credit training for West and Central Africa and also in charge of graduate development for Africa. He headed the Corporate Banking Directorate in Ghana and also in Sierra Leone. He also held roles in Retail Banking including Branch Operations Manager. Mr. Atsu also serves as a consultant and his interests are in Banking and Business Capacity Development. He has consulted for Sierra Leone Commercial Bank, Data Bank, Universal Merchant Bank and Heritage Bank. He is also an adjunct lecturer in Project Management at Accra Institute of Technology. He also consults for Timber companies. He has expertise in Structuring Corporate Banking Business.

    Mr. Atsu holds a Post Graduate Certificate in Sustainability and Environmental Management Systems, Ghana University Technology, Ghana; Master of Science in Business Engineering and Management Systems, Coventry University; Post Graduate Diploma in Business Administration, University of Leicester and Post Graduate Certificate in Management, Nottingham University. He has professional certification in Banking from National Banking College, Manchester Business School, UK and Chartered Institute of Bankers. He has also attended Corporate Banking workshops and short courses in the UK and Hong Kong.

  • ALHAJI ABDUL MUMUNI ADAMU

Alhaji Abdul Mumuni also holds advanced Level French proficiency Certificate.
  • BATHOLOMEW KWAME AHADZI
    Member

    Mr. Bartholomew Kwame Ahadzi is a Chartered Accountant by profession and has over 25 years of working experience. He started his career with Shell Ghana as an Auditor and later joined Coopers and Lybrand as Audit Senior/Consulting Supervisor. He also worked at Ecobank Ghana as Head, Audit Control, The Trust Bank, Ghana as Chief Accountant responsible for Treasury Operations, Financial Controller, Allied Bank Uganda, General Manager Financial Services, Coop Bank, Uganda and HFC Bank, Ghana as Head, Internal Controls. Mr. Ahadzi was also Managing Director for Bank of Africa Kenya and Uganda.

    Mr. Ahadzi is a consultant and has worked on many projects including preparing GHL operationally as it sought to obtain commercial banking license; appointed by Chase Bank Kenya Ltd to assist in extending banking services of Chase Bank Kenya to other East African countries of Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda and South Sudan; Spearheaded the development of a 3-year strategy and set up implementation structures of NMB Bank Ltd, Zimbabwe. He was part of SCG Associates, a consulting firm in Ghana.

    Mr. Ahadzi holds an MBA and BSC Admin, Accounting Options from University of Ghana and a member of the Chartered Institute of Accountants.


    He has attended several international and local learning and capacity development programs in Bank financial and risk management, Advanced Credit, Assets and Liability Management, Human resource management and Leadership and Corporate Governance.
  • ALHAJI ABDUL AZIZ ADAMU IDDRISU

  • ALHAJI ABDOUL ZAKOU ADAMOU
    Member

In 2002, he was part of the training event organized by Ghana Institute of Engineers on the topic Accounting Finance for Project manager and Non-Financial Executives and Contract Administration and Management respectively.
