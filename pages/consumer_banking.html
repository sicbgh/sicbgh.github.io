    TARGET MARKET

    FEATURES

    •   Interest rate is prevailing Base rate plus margin.

    •   Flexible repayment.
    •   Early pay off acceptable.
    •   Competitive interest rate.
    •   Fast and easy.

    •   Fully completed Loan application form.

    •   Three (3) months salary account with TRB.
